Privacy Policy

Wordongo values your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our application works and what data we do not collect.

Data Collection

Wordongo does not collect any personal data from users. The application does not store, share, or process personal information such as name, email, phone number, or location.

Cookies and Local Storage

The application does not use cookies. However, local storage solutions such as SharedPreferences may be used to improve user experience. These data remain on the device and do not contain personal information.

Third-Party Advertising Services

Our application uses Unity Ads to display advertisements. Unity Ads may collect and process certain data, including:

  • Device information (device model, operating system version)
  • Advertising ID
  • IP address
  • Ad interaction data

These data are processed by Unity Ads in accordance with their own privacy policy. For more information, please review Unity Ads’ privacy policy.

Third-Party Services

Other than Unity Ads, no third-party services are used to collect or share user data.

Data Security

Since no personal data is directly collected by the application, there is minimal risk regarding user data security. Data collected by third-party services is handled by those providers.

Children’s Privacy

Wordongo is suitable for all age groups; however, advertisements provided by Unity Ads may display general audience content.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be announced on the Play Store listing.
